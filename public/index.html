Rooms, Suites & Signature Penthouses

A stay at Atlantis The Royal offers unparalleled luxury with its elegant Rooms, Suites, and Signature Penthouses. Rising 43 stories, it combines stylish interiors with stunning views of the Arabian Sea and Palm Island, along with private outdoor spaces and pools. This is a stay you'll show off for years to come.

DUBAI’S PREMIER DINING DESTINATION

Home to 16 world-class restaurants, including 7 celebrity chef restaurants and 1 Michelin starred restaurant, Atlantis The Royal joins Atlantis, The Palm as the top culinary destination in Dubai. Find a selection of some of the finest chefs in the world that will create exceptional dining experiences like no other. From Michelin-starred concepts to gastronomic showstoppers, the world has yet to see the culinary mastery of Atlantis The Royal.

CARBONE DUBAI

CARBONE Dubai brings the golden age of dining back to life. Think tuxedoed Captains, and the unmistakable croon of old-school New York charm. Italian-American classics are reimagined with theatre and every meal feels like a night out in another era.
